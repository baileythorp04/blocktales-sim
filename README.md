## Usage
do not leave or refresh the combat page or else you will lose your progress and get sent back to menu

## Log Settings
#### Hide/Show Boring Logs
boring logs include: dodged attacks, applied status effects, HP or SP gain of 0

#### Highlight Last Action/Current turn
It can either highlight what just happened when you last chose an action to do, Or everything that has already happened in the current turn. You get to make two actions per turn.

#### Reverse
Log either scrolls up or down.
